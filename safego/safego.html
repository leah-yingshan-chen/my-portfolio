Project Overview


SafeGo is a travel app designed to empower solo female travelers with essential safety tools and resources. It features an interactive safety map for secure route planning, an AI- driven travel assistant offering travel advice and emergency support, and a community for sharing tips and experiences.

Ah-uh Moment

üëÄAdopt a broader perspective

In the initial design, I made the "SOS" button highly prominent to ensure users could quickly locate and use it. However, one night I suddenly realized ‚Äî if the user could easily spot it, so could a potential attacker. Such an obvious SOS signal might provoke the perpetrator, putting women in even greater danger.

After realizing this, I shifted my design focus from making the SOS button as noticeable as possible to ensuring it was both discreet and accessible ‚Äî easy for users to activate without drawing attention.

This experience taught me that design is multi-dimensional. While we emphasize being "user-centered," we must also adopt a broader perspective. As designers, creating truly practical solutions requires balancing user needs with potential risks.
